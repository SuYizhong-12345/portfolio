Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

Protagonist: Miles Morales

An intelligent yet rebellious teenager of African-American and Puerto Rican descent, who is imbued with spider-like abilities after being bitten by a mutated spider and eventually takes up the mantle of a masked vigilante named "Spider-Man".

Miles's characteristics

  1. He got physical strength beyond ordinary people
  2. He is good at close combat
  3. He has never been specially trained in combat, which makes him lack of strategy in combat
